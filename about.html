Building robust systems through code, collaboration, and continuous learning

        Engineering continues to inspire my curiosity every day, encouraging me to learn new technologies, experiment

        I am currently looking for opportunities where I can apply my technical skills, contribute to meaningful
        projects, and continue learning from experienced teams. I am open to internships, collaborations, and roles that
        allow me to grow as an engineer while solving real-world problems through technology.

        What makes my journey unique is my curiosity-driven approach to learning. From writing my first programs in
        Python to exploring Machine Learning and Web Development, I have always focused on understanding how technology
        can be used to create practical solutions and improve the way we solve problems.
